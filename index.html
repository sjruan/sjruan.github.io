About

    I am currently a fourth-year Ph.D. student at School of Computer Science and Technology, Xidian University advised by Prof. Yu Zheng.

    I have published some papers in refereed journals and conferences, such as TKDE, KDD, AAAI, ICDE, WWW and UbiComp.

    I also serve as a reviewer/PC member for several referred journals and conferences, e.g., TIST, AAAI, CIKM and MDM.

    My research interests include trajectory data mining, urban planning, spatio-temporal data management and urban computing.
